## First whole year at University of Waterloo

### 1A term

#### Course Load

I have taken following courses in my first term at UWaterloo:

|Course Code|Course Description|
|:---:|---|
|ARTS 190|First-Year Topics in Arts Disciplines|
|ECE 105|Classical Mechanics|
|ECE 150|Fundamentals of Programming|
|ECE 190|Engineering Profession and Practice|
|ECE 198|Project Studio|
|MATH 115|Linear Algebra for Engineering|
|MATH 117|Calculus 1 for Engineering|

I honestly didn't enjoy the term. Not that the courses were super hard, but there was a big bump in workload in comparison to secondary school.
My favorite courses this term were ECE 150 and ECE 198. ECE 150 was basically learning things I already knew fundamentally. ECE 198 was learning how to make actual products and things that can work in the real world.
Obviously, my least favorite courses were MATH 117 and ARTS 190. And I will not explain why.

#### Residence and Food

I was placed in WEN north, right next to my don's room.
Thankfully, I had a kitchen in my residence room, so I was able to cook my own meals. However, since it was mandatory to buy a meal plan, I used the cafeteria for breakfast, snacks, and late supper. My go-to was Booster Juice and pasta; sometimes burgers and pizza when I was up late.
The room was not bad, and the heating was super nice. However, there was no AC whatsoever, so I had to shower multiple times a day.
Also, the washrooms and showers were shared, so sometimes I had to run to my friend's room when I needed a quick shower.

#### Gears

In my 1A term, I used an iPad Pro 11-inch with the M1 chip for my note-taking. I had been using a MacBook Pro 13-inch (2018, 4-port base model).

However, during my finals week, my laptop decided to stop working, so I had to buy a new MacBook Pro 16-inch M1 Pro base model. It was the only available option at the moment, and I had no choice.
