## First whole year at University of Waterloo

### 1A term

#### Course Load

I have taken following courses in my first term at UWaterloo:

|Course Code|Course Description|
|:---:|---|
|ARTS 190|First-Year Topics in Arts Disciplines|
|ECE 105|Classical Mechanics|
|ECE 150|Fundamentals of Programming|
|ECE 190|Engineering Profession and Practice|
|ECE 198|Project Studio|
|MATH 115|Linear Algebra for Engineering|
|MATH 117|Calculus 1 for Engineering|

I honesty didn't enjoy the term. Not that the course was super hard, but there was a big bump in workload in comparison to secondary school.
My favorite course this term was ECE 150 and ECE 198. ECE 150 was basically learning things I know officially. ECE 198 was learning how I make actual product and things that can work in actual world.
Obviously my least favorite course was MATH 117 and ARTS 190. And I will not explain why.

#### Residence and Food

I was place in WEN north, next to my don's room.
Thankfully I had my kitchen in my residence room, I was able to cook my own meal. However, it was mandatory to buy a meal plan for me, I used the cafeteria for breakfast, snacks and late supper. My go-to was booster juice and pasta; sometimes burgers and pizza when I was up late.
The room was not bad, and heating was super nice. However, there was no AC what so ever, I had to shower myself multiple times a day.

#### Gears

In my 1A term, I used iPad Pro 11inch with M1 for my note taking.
I have been using MacBook Pro 13 from 2018 4 port base model.

During my finals week, my laptop decided to stop working so I had to buy new MacBook Pro 16 M1 Pro base model.
It was only available option at the moment and I had no choice.
